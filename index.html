The Property

Our oasis is nestled within the natural beauty of South Lombok. The proximity to Dondon Beach, just a short 800-metre walk away, offers our guests a wonderful opportunity to immerse themselves in the stunning coastal environment. Whether it's lounging on the beach, surfing the waves, or exploring the surrounding area.

Our resort's convenient location ,being just 20 minutes away from Kuta Lombok and the airport makes it easily accessible for travellers.

Having the swimming pool situated in the heart of the glamping area gives an easy access to all guests, and enhances the experience of staying at our property.

The convenience of being able to enjoy an early morning swim or a leisurely afternoon by the pool on one of our beautiful daybeds will add to the relaxation and enjoyment of your stay.

Our breathtaking view from the top of the property for sunrise or sunset will add magic and allure to your experience by providing moments of beauty, tranquillity, and connection with nature.

The Restaurant

Nestled in the heart of our property lies a rustic haven for food enthusiasts - our open-air restaurant, where the warm embrace of wood and the gentle sway of alang-alang roofs welcome you to a unique dining experience. Surrounded by the lush greenery of our gardens, immerse yourself in nature's embrace as you indulge in a culinary journey unlike any other.

At our restaurant, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse selection of menus catering to different dietary preferences. Whether you're a committed vegan, a devoted vegetarian, or a seafood lover embracing the pescatarian lifestyle, there's something special waiting for you here. Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond just the plate; we source our ingredients directly from our very own eco garden and support the livelihood of our local village fishermen, ensuring that every dish is a celebration of freshness and community.

Sustainability

Our property welcomes eco-conscious travellers seeking both luxury and sustainability while relaxing in South Lombok's natural surroundings.

By using traditional building materials , we added to the authenticity of the place but also helped in preserving local culture and reducing environmental impact.

The incorporation of a luxurious tropical garden adds another layer of beauty to our property. We integrated some waste water gardens into the landscape, showcasing our commitment to eco-friendly practices.
