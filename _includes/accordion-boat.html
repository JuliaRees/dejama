Catch a fast boat from Bali or the Gili Islands

There are many companies offering fast boat services from Bali to Lombok with all boats departing between 8.30 and 11.00am.

We recommend catching an early boat as the sea is normally calmer.

You will be picked up at your hotel in Bali between 7-8am and go directly to Serangan Harbour or Padang Bai Harbour, which is a 2.5-hour boat ride to Lombok. For this service we recommend Blue water Express and Ekajaya Fast Ferry.

We can organise our driver to pick you up from Bangsal Harbour and help you with your luggage. It is a scenic 2 hour drive to our resort.
